#  Best practices visualizations

```
- Always ensure that proportions are respected ;
- Use clear legends ;
- Display non-responses to avoid implying that your sample is complete ;
- Use a continuous y-axis, or clearly display the data break on the chart.
- Maintain proportions in your visuals, even if values are indicated.
- A downward arrow will be interpreted by everyone as a decrease, 
  be careful when using this type of shape on your charts.
```
